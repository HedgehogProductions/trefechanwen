Local Facilities

Goodwick — post office, Tesco express, coffee shops and pubs.

Fishguard — Co–Op, bank, post office, shops, pubs, cafes, leisure centre and tourist information. The Theatr Gwaun, in Fishguard, is a community run venue for films and regular live events.

Pembrokeshire’s county town, Haverfordwest, is about 16 miles away and has a range of shops and supermarkets.

Asda and Tesco deliver groceries locally. There are tourist information centres in Fishguard and St David’s.

Eating Out

There are several places to eat out in the local area. Fish and chips are available in Goodwick and Fishguard, as are pubs and cafes. Our guests frequently recommend places to enjoy a meal out in the visitor book in the Cottage and some local hostelries get mentioned on a regular basis. Dog friendly eating places are available too.

Local Festivals

There are several festivals and special events in the local area. The Fishguard Folk festival in May, Pembrokeshire Fish week in June, Pembrokeshire County Show and Cardigan River and Food festival in August are just a few. A list is included in the information folder in the cottage.
