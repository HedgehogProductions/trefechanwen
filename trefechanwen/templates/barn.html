Terms & Conditions

Charges for electricity and oil are included in the prices shown.

Discounts available: A discount of £50 will be applied for parties of 1 or 2 people OR returning guests will be allowed a 10% reduction.

Short Breaks: Short breaks are available all year apart from school summer holidays. The minimum stay is for three nights; please contact us for prices. Discounts do not apply to short breaks.

Payment: Bookings are to be accompanied by a deposit of one third of the rent. The balance of the rent must be paid 56 days (8 weeks) before the commencement of the holiday. The full amount is payable if the arrival date is less than 56 days (8 weeks) from the date of booking.

Cancellation: In the event of cancellation, please advise us immediately. We will attempt to re–let the property and return your payment, less a handling charge of £25.00. If we are unable to re–let all monies will be forfeited, so cancellation protection insurance is recommended. In the unlikely event of the property becoming unavailable due to circumstances outside our control, a complete refund will be paid.

Dogs: Two dogs are welcome FREE. There is a charge of £15 per week for additional dogs. Dogs must not be allowed on furniture or beds and they must have their own bed/blankets to protect the floor coverings. They should be up to date with flea and worming treatments and owners are responsible for cleaning up any mess. Dogs must not be left alone in the property at any time.

Arrival & Departure: The property is available from 4:00 p.m. on the day of arrival and should be vacated by 10:00 a.m. on the day of departure. These times can be varied for winter 'short breaks', just let us know what you would like.

Bed linen & Towels: Beds are fully made up and ready for your arrival. Kitchen and bath towels are provided, but linen for the travel cot is not.

The Barn - Access Statement

Exposure: The barn sits in a courtyard of former farm buildings. The rear garden backs onto a quiet no-through lane.

Transport + Parking: The site is within a mile of Goodwick town (shop, post office, pharmacy and railway station). There is level vehicular access and parking right to the front door.

Front entrance door is reached directly from the gravelled and level parking area, with one slate step up. It is 92cm wide with manual, colour–contrasted door handle at 105cm height and outside light.

Rear entrance door is reached across a gravelled path at the side of the barn which leads to the garden gate. The path is 90cm wide at its narrowest points while the gate is 100cm wide with a latch at 128cm above ground level. From there, a level block-paved path and one slate step give access to the rear door which is 80cm wide with manual, colour–contrasted door handle 100cm high and outside light.

All rooms are on ground–floor level with no steps or slopes. All doors are inward–opening with colour–contrasted handles 102cm above floor level. All light switches are 116cm above floor level and all power points, apart from those above kitchen units, are 50cm above floor level.

Kitchen surfaces and power points are at traditional cabinet height as is the cooking hob.

Reception room is open–plan and consists of the kitchen, dining and seating areas and has six double power points in addition to those in the kitchen.

Bedroom 1 is a large room accessed via a door 82cm wide and there is wheelchair access to both sides of the double bed. There are three double power points.

En suite bathroom, off bedroom 1 has a door 67cm wide with a wheelchair accessible toilet and large, level walk-in shower, neither with support rails.

Bedroom 2 is a compact room accessed via a door 66cm wide and has three double power points.

Bathroom is accessed by a door 66cm wide and has a corner toilet unit without support rails. The shower is over a standard bath which is not wheelchair accessible.

Rear garden has a level block–paved path and seating area. A short slope rises to a level grassy area with fencing and boundary bank.

Information folder includes user instructions for the appliances provided and details of local facilities and destinations.

Miscellaneous:. Owners live on site. Postcode is SA64 0HS but SatNav is likely to take you beyond the premises. There are the ruins of a Welsh Longhouse adjacent to the barn so care is needed with children as it is unstable. The drive from the road is shared with owners and their neighbours. Mobile phone signal is generally good; internet hub details are provided. Electricity and water are mains–supplied; cooking is by electricity; underfloor heating by oil boiler.
