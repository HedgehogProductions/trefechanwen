Lounge & Dining Area

A cosy lounge area with high beamed ceiling with exposed timbers, feature stone fireplace with wood-burning stove and comfortable seating for 4. Includes a 32″ flat screen TV with DVD player, music system, a well stocked bookcase, games and a wealth of tourist information.
The dining area has a table with 4 chairs. A high chair is available on request.
Stable doors lead to a garden and courtyard.

Kitchen

A good sized, well equipped room, with fitted units, electric oven, gas hob, microwave, fridge/ freezer and washing machine. Tea towels are provided.
The cottage has oil fired central heating with radiators in all rooms.

Bedroom

A downstairs bedroom with twin beds, wardrobe and well stocked book case. On request the two beds can be pushed together to make a large double bed — just let us know.
A travel cot (size????) is available on request.

Crog Loft

Double bedroom in a galleried crog loft, snug under the rafters with sloping ceilings. This room is reached via a narrow spiral staircase (with stairgates) from the lounge.
Except for the travel cot all bedlinen is provided and is washed in chemical free detergent.

Bathroom

The bathroom is on the ground floor with toilet, washbasin and bath with a shower over. Bath and hand towels are provided (but not beach or dog towels.)

Garden

To the rear of the cottage is a sheltered, enclosed garden with seating area and garden furniture. To the front of the cottage is ample parking for cars. Attached to the cottage is a lockable outhouse which has a tumble drier and space for storing bikes, wetsuits etc.
N.B. there are the ruins of a traditional stone Welsh longhouse in the front courtyard — care with children, please. The front courtyard is shared with the owners.

Terms & Conditions

Winter Fuel Charge: oil central heating, electricity and gas inclusive May to Sept. £25 per week outside these dates. Free starter basket of logs.

Discounts available: A discount of £50 will be applied for parties of 1 or 2 people OR Returning guests will be allowed a 10% reduction.

Short Breaks: Short breaks are available all year excluding Christmas and New Year. During the winter weeks (beginning of November to end of February) prices are as follows - up to 3 nights - £259, up to 4 nights - £299, up to 5 nights - £339. For other times, please contact us for prices. Small Party Discounts do not apply to short breaks.

Payment: Bookings are to be accompanied by a deposit of one third of the rent. The balance of the rent must be paid 56 days (8 weeks) before the commencement of the holiday. The full amount is payable if the arrival date is less than 56 days (8 weeks) from the date of booking.

Cancellation: In the event of cancellation, please advise us immediately. We will attempt to re-let the property and return your payment, less a handling charge of £25.00. If we are unable to re-let all monies will be forfeited, so cancellation protection insurance is recommended.

In the unlikely event of the cottage becoming unavailable due to circumstances outside our control, a complete refund will be paid.

Dogs: 2 dogs are welcome FREE. There is a charge of £15 per week for additional dogs. Dogs must not be allowed on furniture or beds and they must have their own bed/blankets to protect the floor coverings. They should be up to date with flea and worming treatments and owners are responsible for cleaning up any mess. Dogs must not be left alone in the cottage at any time.

Arrival & Departure: The cottage is available from 4:00 p.m. on the Saturday of arrival and should be vacated by 10:00 a.m. on the day of departure. These times can be varied for winter 'short breaks', just let us know what you would like.

Bed linen & Towels: Beds are fully made up and ready for your arrival. Kitchen and bath towels are provided, however cot linen is not.
