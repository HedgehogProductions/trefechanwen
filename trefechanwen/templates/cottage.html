The Cottage - Access Statement

Exposure: The cottage sits in a courtyard of former farm buildings. The rear garden backs onto an open field.

Transport + Parking: The site is within a mile of Goodwick town (shop, post office, pharmacy and railway station). There is level vehicular access and parking right to the front door.

Front entrance door is reached across a level block-paved path and two block steps. It is 90cm wide with manual, colour-contrasted door handle at 125cm height and outside light.

Rear entrance door is reached across a level block-paved path and one block step. It is 80cm wide with manual, colour-contrasted door handle at 125cm height and outside light.

All rooms — apart from the crog–loft bedroom 1 — are on ground–floor level with no steps or slopes and with inward–opening doors 80cm wide.

Kitchen surfaces and power points are at traditional cabinet height as is the cooking hob. Two double power points at 15cm above floor level.

Bathroom has a wheelchair accessible toilet without support rails. Shower is over a standard bath and is not accessible by wheelchair.

Bedroom 2 is wheelchair accessible and furniture can be rearranged (e.g. twin beds pushed together as double bed) on prior request.

Crog–loft bedroom 1 has limited headroom and is accessed via a narrow 11–stepped spiral staircase which may prove problematic to guests of limited mobility.

Rear garden has level block–paved path and generally level seating area. Gravel paths run round either side to the front of the cottage. Grassy area has gentle slopes within the boundary banks.

Cottage information includes user instructions for appliances provided and details of local facilities and destinations.

Miscellaneous: Owners live on site. Postcode is SA64 0HS but SatNav is likely to take you past the premises. There are the ruins of a Welsh Longhouse adjacent to the front courtyard so care is needed with children. The drive from the road is shared with owners and their neighbours. Mobile phone signal is generally good; internet hub details are provided. Electricity and water are mains–supplied; cooking is by electricity; heating by oil boiler and log burner.
